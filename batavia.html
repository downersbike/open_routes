Short route to Batavia then up the Fox River trail to Geneva and Cafe 318 Same as short route to Batavia but extends by going up the Fox river trail. Suitable more for slower speeds as the trail can get quite crowded. 318 cafe is a great spot with good coffee and gelato. 67.7/42
Route to Batavia using Warrenville and Ferry then ET Tri course, into Geneva and back via Mack. Route to Batavia via Ferry including the overpass hump climb. refuel and bathroom stop at 7/11 in Batavia before carefully riding through town. Ride between deerpath road and Bliss is nasty.. 50mph and narrow.. but worth it! Bliss is.. well bliss.. a stunner of a road. Take Western ave into Geneva for a Latte and Gelato. Route uses Fox river trail to warm up then Mack road return (NOTE: Brief riders about taking the trail when Mack swings to the left or they will get lost) 94.4/58.7
